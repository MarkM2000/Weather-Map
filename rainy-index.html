How Rain Impacts The Environment

Rain is crucial for environmental sustainability as it replenishes water sources like rivers, lakes, and aquifers, supporting plant life and providing freshwater for humans and animals; however, excessive or poorly managed rainfall can lead to negative impacts like flooding, soil erosion, and water pollution due to stormwater runoff, which can significantly harm ecosystems and infrastructure if not properly addressed.
