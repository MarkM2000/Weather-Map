The Impact Of Clouds On The Environment

Clouds have a complex and important role in regulating the climate because they can both cool the planet by reflecting sunlight back into space and warm it by trapping heat radiated from the Earth's surface. The overall effect of clouds varies depending on their type, altitude, and the amount of sunlight present.
